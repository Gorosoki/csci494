Large Group

'Large Group' or 'IV' is where we gather as a whole community each week. We meet on Thursday nights at 7:30pm upstairs in the SOB Barn behind North Hedges Suites for worship through music, prayer, and a speaker who teaches us how to better serve our Lord. Fellowship continues afterward with a hangout. 'Large Group' is a great time to meet people in InterVarsity and to find out what we are about. We would love to see you there!
