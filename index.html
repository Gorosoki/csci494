InterVarsity at Montana State University

   We are located on the campus of Montana State University in beautiful Bozeman Montana, where we have been for over 50 years! InterVarsity is a Christian community for students who have a desire to find a higher purpose for their years at MSU. Despite the name, InterVarsity is not a sports thing. Really, it's a big group of friends who are on a journey to pursue Jesus together. There are tons of ways to get involved in InterVarsity, and we encourage people to stop by any of our activities to just check us out. So if you're interested in pursuing things like friendship, community, purpose, fun, or faith, then consider putting aside your plans for a couple of hours and visit one of InterVarsity's many offerings. At the very least, you'll meet some cool people. Then again, it could make a colossal impact on your college experience at MSU.
